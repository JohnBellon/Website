Genre: Roguelite

"Defy the god of the dead as you hack and slash out of the Underworld in this rogue-like dungeon crawler from the creators of Bastion, Transistor, and Pyre."

Hades developed by Supergiant Games is a masterclass of roguelite gameplay and is a defining cornerstone of the genre. Players take on the role of Zagreus, the rebellious son of Hades, as he attempts to escape the Underworld with the help of Olympian gods, each offering unique boons that shape gameplay. By blending fast paced combat over multiple levels with rich storytelling, and constant progression mechanics that leave you craving another run, the game will quickly immerse you in its dynamic experience. A unique twist on many classical myths, Hades features a rich excellently designed cast of characters familiar to lovers of Greek mythology.

The combat is fluid and satisfying, with a variety of weapons and abilities that encourage experimentation. The game's art style is stunning, featuring hand-drawn character designs and beautifully rendered environments. Its narrative stands out in the roguelike genre, delivering meaningful character interactions and an evolving story that unfolds naturally with each run. And with its incredible voice acting, atmospheric soundtrack, and rewarding gameplay loop, Hades is an outstanding game that keeps players engaged whether they’re chasing a perfect run or uncovering every detail of its rich mythology.
