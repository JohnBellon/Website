Genre: Stealth Sandbox

“Enter the world of the ultimate assassin. HITMAN World of Assassination delivers the definitive stealth action experience, putting you in the shoes of Agent 47, a master assassin. Explore immersive locations, uncover hidden secrets, and eliminate targets with complete freedom of approach.”

Hitman 3 is a great conclusion to IO Interactive’s World of Assassination trilogy, delivering some of the most intricate and creative levels in the franchise. Each mission is a thoroughly thought out and designed sandbox, allowing the player to go after their targets in countless different ways. The well designed maps provide numerous different disguises, routes, and mission stories, offering seemingly unlimited freedom of choice so that no two playthroughs are alike.

You play as the iconic bald-headed Agent 47, a genetically engineered assassin that seeks to uncover the truth about his origins while dismantling the secretive organizations that created and controlled him. The story, while taking a darker and more personal turn for Agent 47, ties up the trilogy’s narrative in a satisfying way. The game's replayability is immense, with challenges, unlockable equipment, and different mission approaches encouraging multiple playthroughs and dozens more hours of gameplay even after you’ve completed the main story. In conclusion, Hitman 3 is an exceptional stealth game that rewards creativity and patience, making it a fitting sendoff for this chapter of Agent 47’s career.
